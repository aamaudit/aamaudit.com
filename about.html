Welcome To Ali Al Mohairy Auditing

Delivering financial accuracy and integrity for over two decades.. Our legacy is marked by unwavering dedication to providing clear and concise financial oversight.

Ali Al Mohairy Auditing accomplishes this huge task not only by providing timely, efficient, and accurate information on matters within its scope but also by assisting in making crucial management and financial decisions in good time and with sound judgment. Ali Al Mohairy Auditing was established by Mr. Ali Sultan Almheiri in 1984. Ali Al Mohairy Auditing is committed to following the disclosure requirements as mandated by law and international standards. Our staff is made up of highly qualified professionals whose expertise is developed and updated through international training, workshops, and seminars. Currently, our professional services are extended to more than 250 active clients, and certified accounts are accepted by the Central Bank of the UAE, other major banks in the UAE, as well as international banks.
